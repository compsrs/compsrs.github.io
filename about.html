About Us

SRS Accounting & Taxation is a CA Firm in Lohegaon, India. The Firm’s practice is being engaged by a team of qualified professionals and associate firms.


The firm is having its own fully furnished office situated at Rajendra Niwas, Narayanwadi, Near Kulkarni Hospital, Chalisgaon, Dist – Lohegaon – 424101 with the latest and high standard infracture with all common measures. Our team comprises of CAs, Semi qualified employees & articles. The firm is also having association with Advocates, Cost Accountants and CS etc. for rendering legal, company law matters & FEMA related services.

We at SRS, provide Accounting services at your doorstep. Important services rendered by our firm include Auditing, Taxation (Income Tax, GST), Registration of Partnership Firms & Companies, Accounts writing, and other financial services.
